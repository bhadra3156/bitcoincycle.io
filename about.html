About Us

We build free, data-driven tools to help the Bitcoin community understand market cycles and make more informed decisions.

What We Do

BitcoinCycle.io is a free Bitcoin cycle analysis dashboard centred on the 1064/364 expansion–compression model. The model identifies two alternating phases in every Bitcoin market cycle: a 1,064-day expansion phase (ATL to ATH) and a 364-day compression phase (ATH to ATL), totalling a 1,428-day full cycle.

We anchor all predictions to the 21 November 2022 FTX cycle bottom — the most clearly defined macro low in recent history — and project cycle dates forward to 2050.

Our Mission

We believe most retail investors are navigating Bitcoin's volatility without the tools to understand where they are in the cycle. Our goal is to make that information free, visual, and always up to date.

Everything on this site — the live countdown, the cycle table through 2050, the calculator, the chart, and the blog — is provided at no cost. We are supported by affiliate partnerships with OKX and Hyperliquid, clearly labelled on the homepage.

Nothing on this site is financial advice. The 1064/364 model is a mathematical framework based on historical cycle patterns. Past cycles do not guarantee future performance. Always do your own research.
