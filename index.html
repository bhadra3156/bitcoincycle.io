What is the Bitcoin Cycle?

A Bitcoin cycle is a recurring pattern of price expansion and compression tied to market psychology and supply dynamics. The 1064/364 model predicts each Bitcoin cycle lasts 1,428 days total — 1,064 days of expansion from ATL to ATH, followed by 364 days of compression from ATH to ATL — anchored to the November 2022 FTX cycle bottom.
